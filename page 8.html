Admissions at Orion Heights University

Application Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Original High School Transcript or College Transcripts
  • Birth Certificate
  • Government Issued ID
  • Passport-sized Photos (2 pieces)
  • Recommendation Letters (if applicable)
  • Entrance Exam Results (for certain programs)

How to Apply

  • Step 1: Fill out the online application form on our website.
  • Step 2: Upload all required documents to your application account.
  • Step 3: Take the entrance exam (if required) or wait for the interview schedule.
  • Step 4: Wait for the acceptance notification and further instructions on enrollment.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Entrance Exam: July 5, 2025
  • Orientation: August 1, 2025
  • Classes Begin: August 10, 2025
