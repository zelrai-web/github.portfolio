University Events 2025

January – New Year Kick-Off

Date: January 8, 2025

A fresh start celebration welcoming back students and staff with activities, games, and goal-setting workshops.

Date: February 14-16, 2025

Experience the spirit of Orion Heights with food booths, club showcases, talent shows, and fun rides.

March – Women’s Month Seminar

Date: March 10, 2025

Empowerment talks, panel discussions, and exhibits celebrating the contributions of women in society.

April – Research Symposium

Date: April 25, 2025

Presentations from students and faculty showcasing innovations, studies, and academic projects.

Date: May 15-16, 2025

Company booths, job matching, resume reviews, and industry talks for graduating students.

Date: June 10, 2025

Official welcome and briefing for incoming freshmen and transferees with campus tours and info sessions.

Date: July 5-6, 2025

Exhibits and demos of student tech projects and industry innovations.

Date: August 20, 2025

A university-wide celebration with parades, recognition ceremonies, and fun competitions.

September – Cultural Festival

Date: September 18, 2025

Explore diverse traditions through performances, cuisine, and exhibits by student cultural groups.

October – Mental Health Awareness Week

Date: October 7-11, 2025

Workshops, open counseling, and wellness activities promoting student mental well-being.

November – Environmental Action Day

Date: November 22, 2025

Tree-planting, coastal clean-up, and sustainability lectures to promote environmental responsibility.

December – Year-End Gala Night

Date: December 15, 2025

Formal night of celebration with dinner, awards, and performances to close the year in style.
