Our Courses

Orion Heights University offers a variety of programs designed to prepare students for success in their chosen fields. Whether you're pursuing a career in technology, business, or the arts, we have a program to fit your needs.

Computer Science

Our Computer Science program prepares students for the ever-evolving tech industry with hands-on learning in programming, algorithms, and software engineering.

Business Administration

Equip yourself with the leadership and management skills necessary for success in the global business world. Learn about entrepreneurship, marketing, and finance.

Fine Arts

Our Fine Arts program allows students to explore their creativity through courses in painting, sculpture, photography, and digital media.

Mechanical Engineering

This program prepares students to design, analyze, and build mechanical systems, combining creativity with technical expertise in fields like robotics and thermodynamics.

Psychology

Explore the human mind and behavior with a Psychology degree. Our program includes courses on clinical psychology, human development, and behavioral science research.

Law

The Law program offers students the opportunity to study the legal system, focusing on constitutional law, criminal law, and ethics, to prepare for a career in the legal field.

Biotechnology

Our Biotechnology program equips students with the knowledge and skills to apply biological science in fields like medicine, agriculture, and environmental science.

Environmental Science

Study the relationship between humans and the environment. Our Environmental Science program includes courses on conservation, sustainability, and environmental policy.

Journalism

Prepare for a career in media with a Journalism degree, focusing on news writing, digital journalism, broadcasting, and media ethics.
